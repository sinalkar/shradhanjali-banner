Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ) & Features

1. What is this Shradhanjali Card Maker app?

This Shradhanjali Card Maker is a completely free, privacy-first online tribute card creator and RIP card design application designed to help grieving families. Without requiring any account registration, email sign-ups, or software downloads, user-uploaded photos and memorial details are processed 100% locally in the browser, ensuring absolute privacy for sensitive family data. The application operates as a comprehensive digital condolence card creator and death anniversary poster maker supporting 12 major Indian languages, including Marathi, Hindi, Gujarati, Tamil, Telugu, and English. Grieving families can choose from 11 devotional color themes inspired by traditional Indian rituals, upload a photo of your loved one, customize frames, and instantly download a high-resolution, watermark-free JPG. The output is optimized for sharing on WhatsApp status, Instagram stories, or sending for high-quality offset and flex printing, offering a dignified digital memorial creation process in under five minutes.

2. How to create an elegant funeral card design or tribute card design?

To create an elegant funeral card design or memorial tribute card, simply use our simple mobile invitation card creator. Start by selecting one of our 11 curated color themes, such as Saffron, Marigold, Sunset, or Rose, which are designed to reflect the quiet dignity of Hindu, Christian, or general prayer ceremonies. Next, upload a high-quality photo of your loved one, which is automatically framed inside a traditional golden circular border. Input the required details, including the deceased person's full name, age, date of passing, a personalized condolence message, and details about the upcoming memorial or punyatithi service. The online platform instantly renders a real-time live preview of the banner. Once finalized, you can download the final graphic in multiple custom aspect ratios, including a 9:16 vertical layout for WhatsApp statuses, a 1:1 square format for Instagram posts, or high-resolution dimensions tailored for commercial flex print shops.

3. मोबाईलवर शोक संदेश किंवा श्रद्धांजलि फोटो कसा तयार करावा? (Shradhanjali photo kaise banaye?)

मोबाईलवर शोक संदेश किंवा श्रद्धांजलि फोटो बॅनर (Shradhanjali Photo Editing) तयार करणे आता अतिशय सोपे झाले आहे. आमच्या ऑनलाईन अँड्रॉइड कार्ड मेकर (Android Card Maker) आणि मोबाईल पोस्टर मेकरचा वापर करून तुम्ही कोणत्याही डिझायनरशिवाय किंवा कॉम्पुटर सॉफ्टवेअरशिवाय अवघ्या ५ मिनिटांत विनामूल्य श्रद्धांजली बॅनर बनवू शकता. सर्वप्रथम, तुमच्या मोबाईल गॅलरीमधून दिवंगत व्यक्तीचा एक स्वच्छ फोटो निवडून तो अपलोड करा. त्यानंतर त्यांचे संपूर्ण नाव, वय, निधन झालेली तारीख आणि दशक्रिया विधी किंवा शोकसभेची वेळ व ठिकाण अशी आवश्यक माहिती भरा. आमचा जनरेटर लगेचच तुम्हाला प्रत्यक्ष बदल दाखवणारे बॅनर पूर्वावलोकन (live preview) तयार करून देतो. शेवटी, तुम्हाला आवडणारे सोनेरी (Gold), सूर्यास्त (Sunset) किंवा परंपरागत (Traditional) डिझाइन निवडून 'बॅनर डाउनलोड करा' बटणावर क्लिक करा. हा उच्च-गुणवत्तेचा जेपीजी (JPG) फोटो थेट तुमच्या मोबाईलमध्ये सेव्ह होतो, जो तुम्ही WhatsApp स्टेटस, फेसबुक पोस्ट किंवा छपाईसाठी (Flex Printing) सहज वापरू शकता.

4. Is my personal data and photo uploaded to any server?

No, this Shradhanjali Card Maker is built with a strict privacy-first architecture where 100% of the image generation and data processing happens entirely inside your local web browser. Unlike other online graphic editors or card design websites, we do not have a backend server that stores, collects, or processes your uploaded family photographs, names, dates, or contact details. Your uploaded files are converted into secure local data URLs using the HTML5 FileReader API, and the final poster is rendered directly on your screen using the client-side html2canvas library. This guarantees that your sensitive personal information never leaves your device, providing complete security and peace of mind during a difficult time. Once you close the browser tab, all entered details are cleared, making this a completely private, safe, and secure utility for all users.
